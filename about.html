About Our Church

Discover our story, mission, and the people who make LMPC a loving community

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love others, and serve the world with the transforming power of the Gospel.

Our Vision

To be a church where every person experiences the life-changing love of Jesus Christ and grows in faithful discipleship within a caring community.

Our Values

Grace, Truth, Community, Service, and Worship guide everything we do as we seek to follow Christ faithfully in our daily lives.

Our Story

Living Mercy Presbyterian Church was founded in 1952 by a small group of families who felt called to establish a Presbyterian congregation that would serve both the spiritual and practical needs of the growing community.

What began as gatherings in homes quickly grew into a vibrant church family. In 1958, we broke ground on our first sanctuary, and over the decades, we've expanded our facilities to include educational wings, a fellowship hall, and modern worship spaces.

Throughout our history, LMPC has been committed to Reformed theology while embracing contemporary worship styles and innovative ministries. We've weathered challenges, celebrated milestones, and consistently focused on our calling to be Christ's hands and feet in our community.

Today, we continue to grow as a multigenerational church family, welcoming people from all walks of life to experience God's love and grace together.

What We Believe

Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God, sufficient for all matters of faith and practice.

Trinity

We worship one God in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, equal in essence and glory.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone, for the glory of God alone.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is fully God and fully man, who died for our sins and rose again for our justification.

Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and mission.

Future Hope

We believe in the return of Christ, the resurrection of the dead, and eternal life for all who trust in Jesus.
