Growing Faith

Regular giving helps develop trust in God's provision and teaches us to hold material possessions with open hands.

Biblical Perspective on Tithing

The Bible teaches us about the principle of tithing - giving the first 10% of our income back to God through the local church. This practice acknowledges God's ownership of everything and demonstrates our trust in His provision.

"Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this," says the Lord Almighty, "and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it." - Malachi 3:10

We believe in:

  • Proportional Giving: Giving based on what God has given us
  • Cheerful Giving: Giving with joy, not obligation
  • Sacrificial Giving: Giving that requires faith and trust
  • Regular Giving: Consistent, planned giving as an act of worship

New to Giving?

If you're new to tithing or giving, start where you can and let God grow your generosity over time. Even small gifts make a big difference!
