What to Expect

Whether you're visiting for the first time or returning after some time away, we want you to feel comfortable and welcome. Our services typically last about 75 minutes and include a time of singing, prayer, scripture reading, and a message from God's Word.

Come As You Are

No dress code required - wear whatever makes you comfortable

Fellowship Time

Coffee and conversation before and after services

Free Parking

Ministries & Programs

Children's Ministry (Ages 0-12)

Nurturing Young Hearts

Our children's ministry creates a safe, fun, and engaging environment where kids can learn about God's love through age-appropriate activities, Bible stories, and worship.

  • Nursery (0-2 years) - Professional childcare during services
  • Preschool Class (3-5 years) - Interactive Bible lessons and crafts
  • Elementary Class (6-12 years) - Bible study and service projects
  • Children's Choir - Musical worship training
  • VBS (Summer) - Week-long vacation Bible school

"Train up a child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it." - Proverbs 22:6

Youth Ministry (Ages 13-18)

Empowering the Next Generation

Our youth ministry helps teenagers navigate life's challenges while building a strong foundation of faith through relevant teaching, authentic community, and service opportunities.

  • Sunday Youth Group - Weekly gatherings with teaching and activities
  • Wednesday Night Youth - Midweek fellowship and Bible study
  • Youth Retreats - Quarterly weekend getaways
  • Mission Trips - Annual service opportunities
  • Confirmation Class - Preparation for church membership

"Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers." - 1 Timothy 4:12

Adult Ministry

Growing in Faith Together

Our adult ministries provide opportunities for spiritual growth, fellowship, and service at every stage of life, from young adults to seniors.

  • Adult Sunday School - Various classes for different interests
  • Life Groups - Small group Bible studies in homes
  • Marriage Ministry - Support and enrichment for couples
  • Senior Saints - Ministry for older adults
  • Support Groups - Grief, addiction recovery, and other life challenges

"As iron sharpens iron, so one person sharpens another." - Proverbs 27:17

Community Outreach

Serving Our Community

We believe faith is best expressed through action. Our outreach ministries serve both local and global communities, sharing God's love through practical help and compassionate care.

  • Food Pantry - Monthly distribution to families in need
  • Community Garden - Fresh produce for local food bank
  • Prison Ministry - Worship services and mentoring
  • International Missions - Support for missionaries worldwide
  • Disaster Relief - Response team for local emergencies

"Faith by itself, if it does not have works, is dead." - James 2:17
