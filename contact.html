Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect when visiting?

Come as you are! Our services are welcoming and casual. Arrive a few minutes early to find parking and get settled. We'll have friendly greeters to help you feel at home.

What about my children?

Children are always welcome in our services. We also offer nursery care for infants and toddlers, and children's church during the sermon for elementary-aged kids.

Do I need to bring anything?

No need to bring anything - Bibles are provided in the pews, and we'll provide any materials needed for worship or teaching.

How can I get involved?

There are many ways to get connected! Talk to our pastors after service, fill out a connection card, or contact our office to learn about volunteer opportunities and small groups.
